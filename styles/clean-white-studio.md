# Clean White Studio

## Visual intent

A neutral commercial presentation optimized for product clarity, consistent catalogs, marketplaces, and easy visual comparison across a collection.

## Background

Use a seamless neutral white-to-light-gray surface with very subtle tonal depth. The result should read as a photographed studio plane, not a transparent cutout, glowing void, or clipped pure-white mask.

## Lighting

Use broad, soft, neutral light with controlled highlights and a clean, diffused contact shadow. Maintain detail in both white textiles and polished reflective surfaces; do not introduce a colored cast.

## Preserve

Product Lock remains absolute: this style cannot alter the product's geometry, arrangement, color, material, construction, texture, transparency, highlights, or identity details. Improve scene integration only through the background and contact shadow.

## Avoid

Exclude all reference text, logos, brand marks, watermarks, captions, labels, typographic decoration, products, and props. Avoid blown product whites, gray edge halos, floating cutouts, hard mirror shadows, background gradients crossing the product, and decorative studio objects. Output remains text-free unless the user supplies exact text and explicitly requests it.
