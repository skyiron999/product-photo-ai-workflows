# Warm Beige Editorial

## Visual intent

A tactile, contemporary fashion-editorial presentation with warm restraint, gentle depth, and room for the product to feel crafted rather than clinical.

## Background

Use a warm beige matte surface with fine mineral or uncoated-paper character. Keep texture subtle and scale-appropriate. Props are absent by default and may appear only when the user explicitly requests and describes them.

## Lighting

Use gentle directional light, low-to-medium contrast, soft highlight rolloff, and a natural diffused contact shadow. Warm the environment without applying a global warm grade to the product.

## Preserve

Product Lock remains absolute: this style cannot alter the product's geometry, arrangement, color, material, construction, pattern, texture, reflections, or identity details. Neutral and cool product colors must remain neutral or cool even within the warm scene.

## Avoid

Exclude all reference text, logos, brand marks, watermarks, captions, labels, typographic decoration, products, and props. Avoid orange or sepia product casts, rustic clutter, strong canvas texture, dried plants, hard sunlight, and borrowed editorial objects. Output remains text-free unless the user supplies exact text and explicitly requests it.
